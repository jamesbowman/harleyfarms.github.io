Dinners at The Dairy

Dine by candlelight in a hayloft. Reserve our majestic fir table for under-the-radar romance. Our tasty town of Pescadero is known for excellent local foods and cooking, and this will be your intimate celebration of our bounty. Seats twenty, but talk to us about your larger party at events@harleyfarms.com.

Farm dinners

Want just a couple of seats at the table? We host several farm dinners over the year, open to all. Join our mailing list for a reminder when booking opens on November 1st. Do bring your own bottle or two, but please leave your children under 18 years at home.
