Private Events

Host your own farm dinner, for an extraordinary evening with family and friends. Bring your team offsite, for the magic of sharing a day on a working farm.

Private dinners

Real food on a real farm. Host a dinner with us. Your party will meet the goats, nibble hors d’oeuvres in the garden, and unwind as dusk falls over the fields with local foods served by candlelight in our hayloft. Listen to the barn owls from the balcony and know that this is the life. Twenty seats. Email us at events@harleyfarms.com to book your event.
