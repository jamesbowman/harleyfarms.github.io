Farm Shop

Picnic on the farm or take home a souvenir of Pescadero. The farm shop is in the old dairy stables, with horse hoof grooves in the floor. Ask our staff for a taste of anything you fancy and stock your pantry with some farm-made goodness.

Take home our farmstead cheeses, farm honey, and jams, jellies and vinegars. Treat yourself with goat cheese ravioli, truffles, and chocolate pudding. Look after yourself with our delicately scented goat milk soaps and lotions. And if you can't come back soon, shop online for all our bath and body loveliness at Harley Farms Body Shop.
