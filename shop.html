Farm Shop

Picnic on the farm or take home a souvenir of Pescadero. The farm shop is in the old dairy stables, with horse hoof grooves in the floor. Ask our staff for a taste of anything you fancy and stock your pantry with some farm-made goodness.

Cheese

We make farmstead dairy cheese, which is cheese from animals raised on our farm. It does not get better than this, which is why the chevre with herbs and flowers holds a World Gold Medal.

Farm Pantry

When you have something delicious in the pantry, you are ready for anything.

Bath and Body

Proper soap and lotions made from our fresh goat milk and other ethically-sourced ingredients. Unscented and delicately scented, for all skins.
