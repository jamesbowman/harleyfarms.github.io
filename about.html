About the farm

The smells, the routine, the noises and energy of the farm fill the land with a hum and a rhythm of the seasons.
The milkers welcome the herd each morning and begin our day. The cheesemakers chitter chatter as they make cheese with meticulous attention to the art. Our shopkeepers and dinner makers weave between the workings of the dairy and the goats and fill our shelves with farm-made foods and heartwarming meals.
